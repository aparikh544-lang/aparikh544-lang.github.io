Second Layer

Welcome to the Second Layer tutorial of Cubing 101! In this section, we will guide you through the steps to solve the second layer of the Rubik's Cube by inserting the colored edges into their correct positions.

Step 1: Locating the Edge Pieces

The first step in solving the second layer is to locate the edge pieces that belong in the middle layer. These edge pieces do not contain the color of the top face (usually yellow) or the bottom face (usually white). Look for edge pieces that have the colors of the side faces.

Step 2: Inserting the Edge Pieces

Once you have identified an edge piece that belongs in the second layer, follow these steps to insert it into its correct position:

Step 2a: Getting the Edge Piece to the Top Layer

If the edge piece is in the middle layer, use any of the algorithms below to bring it to the top layer. If it is already in the top layer, you can skip this step.

Step 2b: Aligning the Edge Piece

Rotate the top layer to position the edge piece directly above the center piece of the same color on one of the side faces. This alignment is crucial for correctly inserting the edge piece into the second layer.

Step 2c: Inserting the Edge

Once you have aligned the edge piece, the color on top will match the center piece on the left or right face. Depending on which side the edge piece needs to go, use one of the following algorithms:

For inserting to the right: U R U' R' U' F' U F
For inserting to the left: U' L' U L U F U' F'

Repeat this process for all the edge pieces until the second layer is complete.
