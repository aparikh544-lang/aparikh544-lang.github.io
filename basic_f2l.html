Basic F2L Algorithms

Welcome to the Basic F2L Algorithms section of Cubing 101! Here, you'll find a collection of fundamental algorithms designed to handle common F2L cases on the Rubik's Cube.

These basic algorithms are intended for cubers who are new to F2L techniques and are looking to build a strong foundation. Below, you'll find a series of steps to help you tackle even the most common F2L scenarios.

Step 1: Find Your Pair

Locate the corner and edge pieces that form the F2L pair you want to solve.

Step 2: Position the Pair

Use basic moves to position the pair above its target slot.

Step 3: Insert the Pair

Execute the appropriate algorithm to insert the pair into its slot.
