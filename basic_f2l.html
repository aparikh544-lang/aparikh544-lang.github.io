Basic F2L Algorithms

Welcome to the Basic F2L Algorithms section of Cubing 101! Here, you'll find a step by step guide to solve any F2L case on the Rubik's Cube.

This procedure is intended for cubers who are new to F2L techniques and are looking to build a strong foundation. Even pros use these steps to solve F2L efficiently.

Step 1: Find Your Pair

Locate the corner and edge pieces that form the F2L pair you want to solve.


Step 2: Position the Pair

Use basic moves to position the pair above its target slot.


Step 3: Insert the Pair

Execute the appropriate algorithm to insert the pair into its slot.
