PLL Algorithms

Welcome to the PLL (Permutation of the Last Layer) Algorithms section of Cubing 101! Here, you'll find a comprehensive list of algorithms to help you master the PLL step in solving the Rubik's Cube.
PLL is a crucial step in the CFOP method, where the goal is to permute the pieces of the last layer so that the entire top face is a single color. This section provides detailed algorithms for each of the 21 possible PLL cases, along with tips and tricks to execute them efficiently.

Explore the algorithms below to enhance your cubing skills and improve your solve times!
