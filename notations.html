Notations

Welcome to the Notations section of Cubing 101! Here, you'll find a comprehensive list of notations used in Rubik's Cube solving.
Notations are essential for understanding and executing algorithms. This section provides detailed explanations of the standard notations used in Rubik's Cube solving, including face turns, rotations, and other conventions.

Explore the notations below to enhance your cubing skills and improve your solve times!

Standard Notations

  • R: Turn the Right face clockwise
  • R': Turn the Right face counterclockwise
  • R2: Turn the Right face 180 degrees
  • L: Turn the Left face clockwise
  • L': Turn the Left face counterclockwise
  • L2: Turn the Left face 180 degrees
  • U: Turn the Upper face clockwise
  • U': Turn the Upper face counterclockwise
  • U2: Turn the Upper face 180 degrees
  • D: Turn the Down face clockwise
  • D': Turn the Down face counterclockwise
  • D2: Turn the Down face 180 degrees
  • F: Turn the Front face clockwise
  • F': Turn the Front face counterclockwise
  • F2: Turn the Front face 180 degrees
  • B: Turn the Back face clockwise
  • B': Turn the Back face counterclockwise
  • B2: Turn the Back face 180 degrees

Wide Moves

  • r: Turn the Right face and the middle slice clockwise
  • r': Turn the Right face and the middle slice counterclockwise
  • r2: Turn the Right face and the middle slice 180 degrees
  • l: Turn the Left face and the middle slice clockwise
  • l': Turn the Left face and the middle slice counterclockwise
  • l2: Turn the Left face and the middle slice 180 degrees
  • u: Turn the Upper face and the middle slice clockwise
  • u': Turn the Upper face and the middle slice counterclockwise
  • u2: Turn the Upper face and the middle slice 180 degrees
  • d: Turn the Down face and the middle slice clockwise
  • d': Turn the Down face and the middle slice counterclockwise
  • d2: Turn the Down face and the middle slice 180 degrees
  • f: Turn the Front face and the middle slice clockwise
  • f': Turn the Front face and the middle slice counterclockwise
  • f2: Turn the Front face and the middle slice 180 degrees
  • b: Turn the Back face and the middle slice clockwise
  • b': Turn the Back face and the middle slice counterclockwise
  • b2: Turn the Back face and the middle slice 180 degrees

Slice Moves

  • M: Turn the middle slice between L and R clockwise
  • M': Turn the middle slice between L and R counterclockwise
  • M2: Turn the middle slice between L and R 180 degrees
  • E: Turn the middle slice between U and D clockwise
  • E': Turn the middle slice between U and D counterclockwise
  • E2: Turn the middle slice between U and D 180 degrees
  • S: Turn the middle slice between F and B clockwise
  • S': Turn the middle slice between F and B counterclockwise
  • S2: Turn the middle slice between F and B 180 degrees

Cube Rotations

  • x: Rotate the entire cube as if performing an R move
  • x': Rotate the entire cube as if performing an R' move
  • x2: Rotate the entire cube 180 degrees as if performing an R2 move
  • y: Rotate the entire cube as if performing a U move
  • y': Rotate the entire cube as if performing a U' move
  • y2: Rotate the entire cube 180 degrees as if performing a U2 move
  • z: Rotate the entire cube as if performing an F move
  • z': Rotate the entire cube as if performing an F' move
  • z2: Rotate the entire cube 180 degrees as if performing an F2 move
