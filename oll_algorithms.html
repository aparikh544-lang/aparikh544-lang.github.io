OLL Algorithms

Welcome to the OLL (Orientation of the Last Layer) Algorithms section of Cubing 101! Here, you'll find a comprehensive list of algorithms to help you master the OLL step in solving the Rubik's Cube.
OLL is a crucial step in the CFOP method, where the goal is to orient all the pieces of the last layer so that the entire top face is a single color. This section provides detailed algorithms for each of the 57 possible OLL cases, along with tips and tricks to execute them efficiently.

Explore the algorithms below to enhance your cubing skills and improve your solve times!
